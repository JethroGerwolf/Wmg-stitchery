Pattern

Cardbaord Cut Out
Quitting Thread
Cross Stitch Needle
Magnet

1. Understand the pattern

There are Three stitches you will need to know to make this pattern.

1. Cross Stitch

2. Back Stitch

3. Running Stitch

The symbols of each stitch

Note the running stitch in not in the pattern so it doesn't have a symbol.

Every pattern has a center.

To find the center of your fabric. Fold it in half twice like this.

Then open it and match the center of the pattern with the fabic

2. Start Stitching

start from the center and work your way out.

Note: do all of your cross stitch before your back stitch.

3. Putting it together

Stack the fabic, batting, and the cardbaord like shown

Flip it over. Take your quitting thread and do a running stitch about 1/2 inch in on the fabric. All the way around.

To finish it go past where you started and do two half hitches or as you might know it a granny knot.

4. Put the magnet on the back and enjoy.
